How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your data, you can do so by following the steps below:

  • Step 1: Log in to your account.
  • Step 2: Go to Profile.
  • Step 3: Click on Delete My Account.
  • Step 4: Select Yes if you want to permanently delete your data and lose all your data.

Note: Following these steps will end up deleting your account and every data saved in the account, and you won't be able to recover the data.
